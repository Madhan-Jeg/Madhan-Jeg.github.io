My Projects

Voter Turnout Predictors

In a team environment, processed and cleaned over 40,000,000 rows of voter data from specific U.S. states using Google Cloud Platform. Explored and visualized variables of interest, then employed linear regression and decision trees to train separate models in order to find the best predictors of voter turnout.

World Happiness Exploration

Identified patterns, correlations, and outliers within the World Happiness Report dataset. Conducted principal component analysis to extract insights about the contributing trends and drivers of country-level happiness scores. Created an interactive dashboard with robust visuals to help users explore the dataset.

Notebook Dashboard

Marine Productivity Analysis

Investigated the relationship between nutrient availability and primary productivity in U.S. bodies of water to draw conclusions about geographic and seasonal trends relevant for assessing ecosystem health. Developed a responsive dashboard with dynamic visuals to assist users in comprehending the dataset.

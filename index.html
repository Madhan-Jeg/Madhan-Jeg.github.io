My Projects

Voter Turnout Predictors

In a team environment, processed and cleaned over 40,000,000 rows of voter data from specific U.S. states using Google Cloud Platform. Explored and visualized variables of interest, then employed linear regression and decision trees to train separate models in order to find the best predictors of voter turnout.

World Happiness Exploration

Identified patterns, correlations, and outliers within the World Happiness Report dataset. Cleaned and visualized key aspects of the data, then conducted principal component analysis to extract insights about the contributing trends and drivers of country-level happiness scores.

Marine Productivity Analysis

Investigated the relationship between nutrient availability and primary productivity, compared nutrient levels across U.S. regions and seasons, and identified anomalies. Manipulated and visualized data to draw conclusions about geographic and seasonal trends relevant for assessing ecosystem health.
